Alduin

And the Scrolls have foretold,
of black wings in the cold,
That when brothers wage war come unfurled!
Alduin, Bane of Kings, ancient shadow unbound,
With a hunger to swallow the world!

  • Race: Dragon
  • Title: Dovahzul, the World Eater
  • Location: Skuldafn

First of his kind, son of Akatosh, he is considered
the « wellspring » of the Nordic pantheon,
as well as the harbinger of the apocalypse,
believed to have a greater role in reshaping the world entirely.
